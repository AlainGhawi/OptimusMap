OptimusMap is designed to optimize camera installation locations for crime and traffic monitoring. By analyzing data and generating heatmaps of hotspots, OptimusMap provides recommendations to clients on where to install cameras within an area, city, or municipality.

📊 Key Features

  • 🌐 Comprehensive analysis of crime and traffic data
  • 🔥 Heatmaps highlighting high-priority areas
  • 📍 Camera location recommendations for optimal coverage

👮 Target Audience

Our primary clients are police departments seeking to enhance their surveillance capabilities through strategic camera placement.

🌟 Benefits

  • 🔒 Improved crime prevention and detection
  • 🚦 Enhanced traffic monitoring and management
  • 📈 Data-driven decisions for camera installations
